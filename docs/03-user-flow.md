# User Flow

1. Open the app in Expo Go.
2. Read the offer: "Reach Level 5 within 7 days and earn 500 coins."
3. Tap `Start Offer`.
4. Confirm the status is active and current level is Level 1.
5. Tap `Play Next Level` to progress one level at a time.
6. Reach Level 5 before or exactly at the deadline.
7. Confirm the wallet balance is 500 coins and reward history shows the granted reward.
8. Use `Reset Test Data` and confirm the alert to clear local test data.
